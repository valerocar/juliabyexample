Consider a mechanical system with one degree of freedom $x = x(t)$ with potential function $U=U(x)$. The law of conservation of energy establishes that $$E(x(t),\dot{x}(t)) = \text{constant for all }t\in R$$ where $$E(x,v) = \frac{1}{2}m v^2 + U(x).$$ The potential energy for a pendulum has the form $$ U(x) = (g/l)\cos(x)$$
